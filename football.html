ZANZIBAR MEDICAL PROJECT

Volunteer in Zanzibar as a medical assistant and help out in hospitals or clinics in the fascinating historic Stone Town.

Minimum Duration: 2 weeks
Cost: Cost €200 per week
Minimum Age: 18 years

Details

In Malawi football and netball has taken the hearts many,there is less investment and attention in other sports.So it is our calling to create an opportunity to develop young generation vision in every sport especially girls.There has been lack of girls participation in sports due to culture and costs required to provide equipment for some sports.Through our volunteer sports projects the girls and children are now able to take part in the sports they love .By bringing your couching skills and your love for sports can create positive physical health ,life skills ,social skills and personal development to these girls and children from poor disadvantaged communities.
If the sport you are interested to couch or take part in is not listed above don’t be discouraged you can let us know which sport you’re interested .

What's Included

Accommodation

During your stay with us, you'll reside in our volunteer home, where each room accommodates up to four individuals, featuring comfortable bunk beds for a restful night's sleep. Our facilities include shared showers and toilets, ensuring convenience for all residents. Additionally, communal areas are available for relaxation, equipped with amenities such as television, games, and Wi-Fi access. For those seeking added privacy, double rooms are available for an extra fee—please reach out to us to inquire about availability.

Meals

We provide three delicious meals a day, ensuring you're well-nourished throughout your volunteering experience.

Transportation

Transportation from Monday to Friday is provided as part of the package, to and from the projects location. However, you are responsible for arranging your own transportation to the volunteer location. Arrival information will be provided to you after booking.

What's Not Included

What's excluded from the package are travel insurance, visas, pocket money, flights, and personal luxuries such as snacks, beverages, and weekend trips expenses. Starting date— You can start any time throughout the year. We encourage you to arrive on a weekend so that you can begin on Monday or Tuesday. If you are arriving earlier, please let us know. Public holidays and weekends are non-working days for the project. Since we provide other projects besides sports, you are welcome to join as well. No certificate or experience is required to coach, but an understanding of the game and fitness are important.
